Sentence Structure

Learn how to form clear and grammatically correct sentences!

ðŸ”¹ What is Sentence Structure?

Sentence structure refers to the way words and phrases are arranged to create complete sentences.

ðŸ”¹ Basic Types of Sentences:

  • Simple Sentence: One independent clause.
    Example: She reads every day.
  • Compound Sentence: Two independent clauses joined by a conjunction.
    Example: She reads every day, and she writes in her journal.
  • Complex Sentence: One independent clause + one or more dependent clauses.
    Example: Although she was tired, she finished the book.
  • Compound-Complex Sentence: Two or more independent clauses + one or more dependent clauses.
    Example: Although she was tired, she finished the book, and she wrote a review.

ðŸ”¹ Tips for Better Sentence Structure:

  • Use correct punctuation.
  • Avoid sentence fragments and run-ons.
  • Vary sentence length and style.
  • Use conjunctions wisely to connect ideas.
