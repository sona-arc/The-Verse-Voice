Conjunctions in English Grammar

Conjunctions are words used to connect clauses or sentences, or to coordinate words in the same clause.

ðŸ”¹ Types of Conjunctions

  • Coordinating Conjunctions: and, but, or, nor, for, so, yet
  • Subordinating Conjunctions: because, although, while, if, since, when
  • Correlative Conjunctions: either...or, neither...nor, not only...but also

ðŸ”¹ Examples

  • I wanted to go, but I was too tired.
  • Although it rained, we went out.
  • Either you leave now, or you stay till the end.
