Tense Structure Example Usage
Simple Present Subject + base verb (s/es) She reads every day. Facts, habits, routines
Present Continuous Subject + is/am/are + verb-ing I am studying now. Actions happening now
Present Perfect Subject + has/have + past participle He has finished his work. Recently completed actions
Present Perfect Continuous Subject + has/have been + verb-ing She has been reading for hours. Actions that started in the past and continue
Simple Past Subject + past form of verb They watched a movie. Completed actions in the past
Past Continuous Subject + was/were + verb-ing We were playing cricket. Ongoing past actions
Past Perfect Subject + had + past participle He had left before I arrived. Earlier past actions
Past Perfect Continuous Subject + had been + verb-ing They had been living there for years. Past duration before another past event
Simple Future Subject + will + base verb I will visit you tomorrow. Future actions or intentions
Future Continuous Subject + will be + verb-ing She will be working at 6 PM. Future actions in progress
Future Perfect Subject + will have + past participle They will have finished by then. Future completed actions
Future Perfect Continuous Subject + will have been + verb-ing He will have been teaching for 10 years. Ongoing future actions with duration
