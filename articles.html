Articles are words used before nouns to define whether something is specific or unspecific.

Types of Articles

  • Definite Article: the
  • Indefinite Articles: a and an

The is used when referring to a specific noun that is known to the reader or listener.

ğŸ“Œ Common Rules & Usage

  • Use the when there is only one of something: the sun, the earth
  • Use the with superlatives: the best, the most beautiful
  • Use the with ordinal numbers: the first, the second
  • Use the with rivers, seas, oceans: the Nile, the Pacific
Note: Articles are not used with uncountable nouns when talking generally: Water is essential. (NOT "A water")
